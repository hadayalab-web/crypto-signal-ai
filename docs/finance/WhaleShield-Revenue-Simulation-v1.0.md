# WhaleShield Initial Revenue Simulation (Month 1-3)
# Model: Global Affiliate (Tier 2 -> Tier 1)
# Product Price: **$99/mo**
# Affiliate Commission: 70% (**$69.30** to Affiliate, **$29.70** to Platform)

## 1. Key Assumptions
- **Active Affiliates:** Starting with 10, growing to 50.
- **Clicks per Affiliate/Day:** 20 (Conservative organic traffic).
- **Conversion Rate (LP -> Sale):** **1.5%** (Adjusted for higher price point).
- **Churn Rate:** 15% (Monthly).

---

## 2. Monthly Projection

### Month 1: "Ignition" (Soft Launch)
- **Affiliates:** 10 active.
- **Total Traffic:** 6,000 clicks.
- **New Subs:** 90 users (1.5% CVR).
- **Gross Revenue:** **$8,910**
- **Affiliate Payout (70%):** -$6,237
- **Net Profit (30%):** **$2,673**
- *Insight:* Even with lower CVR, Net Profit is 50% higher than the $49 model.

### Month 2: "Momentum" (Expansion)
- **Affiliates:** 25 active.
- **Total Traffic:** 15,000 clicks.
- **New Subs:** 225 users.
- **Existing Subs (Retained):** 76 (90 - 15% churn).
- **Total Active Subs:** 301 users.
- **Gross Revenue:** **$29,799**
- **Affiliate Payout:** -$20,859
- **Net Profit:** **$8,940**
- *Goal:* Hit the ~$10k Net Profit mark faster.

### Month 3: "Scale" (Viral Loop)
- **Affiliates:** 50 active.
- **Total Traffic:** 30,000 clicks.
- **New Subs:** 450 users.
- **Existing Subs (Retained):** 256 (301 - 15% churn).
- **Total Active Subs:** 706 users.
- **Gross Revenue:** **$69,894 / month**
- **Affiliate Payout:** -$48,925
- **Net Profit:** **$20,969 / month**

---

## 3. The "Whale" Target (Affiliate Motivation)
To motivate Affiliates, the math is now explosive:
> "Get just **100 subscribers**, and you earn **$6,930 every single month** passively."
> "That is a life-changing income in almost any country."

## 4. Conclusion
- **Break-even:** Immediate.
- **$10k Net Profit:** Achievable by **Month 2**.
- **Strategy:** The $69 recurring commission is an irresistible hook for Super Affiliates. Use this to recruit aggressively.
