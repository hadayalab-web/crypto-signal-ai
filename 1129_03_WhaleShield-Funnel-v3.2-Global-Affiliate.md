## 1. Strategic Overview (Tier 1 & Tier 2)
Our strategy leverages high-commission affiliate networks in emerging markets to capture high-value customers in developed markets.

### Target Segments
- **Tier 1 (Customers):** Traders in USA, UK, Canada, Australia.
  - *Profile:* High disposable income, tired of losing money, seeking "institutional edge."
  - *Product:* WhaleShield Pro ($49/mo).
- **Tier 2 (Affiliates):** Marketers in India, Vietnam, Nigeria, Philippines.
  - *Profile:* Hungry for USD income, skilled in traffic generation, extensive networks.
  - *Incentive:* **70% Recurring Commission** (Unheard of in the industry).

4. **Checkout:** Whop (Handles payments & Telegram access).
5. **Upsell (Future):** "WhaleShield Elite" (Advanced metrics) or "Inner Circle" (Mastermind).
